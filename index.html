Think smart. Stack fast. Win the line.

A modern logic & strategy game with simple rules and endless depth.

Why StackLine?

  • ✅ Simple to learn
  • ✅ Deep strategy with 7 sizes per color
  • ✅ Fast & replayable (10–20 min)

How to Play

  1. Place a piece on an empty cell or cover a smaller one.
  2. Make a line of 3 of your color to win.
  3. Each match = 2 games, both players start once.
